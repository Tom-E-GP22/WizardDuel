Wizard Duel
5 way rock paper scissors but wizards and spells
2D Sideview Action PvP
Each player selects 5 spells, 
same spell can be reused multiple times in one battle.
Once both players have selected their spells the battle begins.
During battle both players have 3 health and the person with 
the most health at the end of battle wins the round. Best of 3.

Players choose from 4 different wizards.
To hinder people from chosing the same wizard, player 1
will have to choose their wizard before getting the key to invite
their oponent. After which the portrait is locked.
